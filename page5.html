Trainers
The term is commonly used as a person who catches. Trainers start at 10 years old. They catch Pokémon with pokeballs. They go to professors for their starting Pokémon. For example, some trainers can pick a grass Pokémon, a water Pokémon, and a fire Pokémon. One trainer known as Ash, got Pikachu a electric type Pokémon as his starting Pokémon. Some trainers go to a league in the region they are in.
